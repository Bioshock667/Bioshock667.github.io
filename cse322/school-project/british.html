British Faction

This faction is introduced the the Opposing Fronts expansion. While the other factions have headquarters set as permanent buildings on opposing sides of the map, the British have mobile headquarters that can deploy almost anywhere. This helps secure control points and deploy your units closer to the enemy. This also puts your headquarters in danger. Losing the HQ truck means spending manpower for another. Overall, I think this faction is the most interesting and fun to play but very difficult to master.
