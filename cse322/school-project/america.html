American Faction

This faction one of the two factions of the original Company of Heroes. The American faction is an easy faction to start with due to many things. The American faction as a well-rounded selection of infantry to chose from with armor not as strong as the Germans' but more mobile and quicker to deploy. One key factor that makes the American faction easy for beginners is the fact the American units earn experience from fighting. Which means units get stronger the more kills they get.
