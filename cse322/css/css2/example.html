Cool Duck Facts

Ducks are water fowl, so they spend most of their time in water areas. They feed on fish, insects and aquatic plants.

Their bills have comb-like structures for dredging through ponds for food.

Ducks have been known to make great pets.

Ducks are also cosmopolitan, which means they are found everywhere outside Antartica.

Also, their meat is a popular delicacy in Asian countries like China.

Ducks also have many predators like birds of prey, such as the Red-Tailed Hawk.
