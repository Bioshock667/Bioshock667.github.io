The Pagan Origins of Halloween

During the pre-Christan era of Europe, the Gaelics would hold a festival at the end of harvest season. During the 9th Century AD, the Catholic Church would adopt the holiday to worship saints and greive for deceased loved ones instead calling it All Hallow's Eve.
