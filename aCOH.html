About Company of Heroes

In 2006, Relic Entertainment would develop a game that would revolutionize the world of Real-Time Strategy gaming by setting their new RTS game in World War II. With experience from developing the Warhammer 40K RTS franchise, they would go on to develop Company of Heroes for PC. The game would receive critical success with good ratings and high sales.

How to Play

Company of Heroes is an RTS game that gives you a bird's-eye-view. You select units on a battlefield by left-clicking or holding left-click then drag the selection rectangle over units to select a group. Right click on the land to send them to a location or attack by right-clicking on the unit.
