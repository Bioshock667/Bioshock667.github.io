Panzer Elite

This faction was also introduced in the Opposing Fronts expansion, meaning that it provides a new style of play like the British. The Panzer Elite has plenty of light vehicles for different proposes as well as there fair share of heavy armor. This encourages some vehicle-based fighting which would prove difficult against anti-tank guns. The Panzer Elite units also gain experience from fighting but the player must choose to increase the unit's defense (more damage resistance) or offense( higher attack power). Overall, this faction is better played with some experience.
