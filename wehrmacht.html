Wehrmacht Faction

This is one of the original factions, so like the Americans it has a fair balance of infantry. But, it has stronger armor that cost more and the Germans gain experience from spending resources on a KampfKraft center. This proves that your success in winning as Wehrmacht involves good resource management. From my experience, a popular Wehrmacht tactic involves capturing as many capture points as possible then defend until you have enough units to push back. Despite this, I would also recommend playing this faction for new players.
