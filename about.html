Welcome to the Home Page!

Welcome dear reader! This website is programmed by Seth Lemanek and it serves as not only a project, but also a commemoration to one of my most favorite games of all time. The template used is Bootstrap. If you happen to come across this website by chance or by a given link, Welcome! If you like or don't like what you see, contact me and give some feedback.

This website was originally going to describe each faction very deeply but due to time constraints and there alerady being a wiki for anyone who wants to know more about the game. I decided each faction page will describe the basics of each and what I think of them.
